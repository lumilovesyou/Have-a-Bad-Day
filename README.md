# Have a Bad Day

*A mod with the sole purpose of making playing the game unbearable.*

Have a Bad Day adds blinking and breathing that the player must manually activate regularly.

</center></div>

## How to use

### Keybinds

Pressing `v` to breathe will refill your oxygen bar unless you're underwater or on fire. Pressing `b` to blink will remove the eye strain overlay and reset the timer until you next need to blink.
